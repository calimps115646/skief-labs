The Problem

Your team is doing work the business
should be doing for them.

As B2B companies grow, something predictable happens.

Tools multiply. Data fragments. Processes that worked at ten people start breaking at thirty. And somewhere along the way, your best people become the glue — manually researching, chasing, reconciling, compensating.

It functions. Until it doesn't.

The businesses we work with aren't failing. They're growing — and their systems haven't kept up. The cost is real: slower decisions, inconsistent outbound, senior time absorbed by work that shouldn't require senior people.

Adding more software doesn't fix this.
Adding more people only delays it.

What changes things is replacing fragile, human-dependent effort with systems that work quietly in the background.

Two problems. Solved seriously.

We don't do operations systems, general automation, or "digital transformation." We work on two specific things with the depth that comes from choosing to go narrow instead of wide.

A note on AI & Automation

You'll notice we don't list AI and automation as a service. That's intentional. AI and automation are how we build — not what we sell. The technology is never the point. The point is the outcome.

This works best for a specific kind of company.

B2B teams — typically 10 to 200 people — where:

  • ✓ Growth has outpaced the systems underneath it
  • ✓ Manual effort increases faster than headcount
  • ✓ Data lives in spreadsheets, inboxes, and people's heads
  • ✓ Leadership wants outcomes, not another tool to manage
  • ✓ There is no internal automation or RevOps function — by choice or reality

If your people are the glue holding your data or outbound together, that's the pattern we fix.
