What this conversation is

It's a diagnostic, not a demo.

We won't walk you through our capabilities or show you what we've built for other people. We'll ask questions about your specific situation — how your business operates, where effort is concentrated, what's breaking — and tell you honestly whether we think we can help.

Sometimes the answer is no. We're comfortable with that.

Either way, you leave with a clearer picture of what's worth fixing — and what isn't.

This conversation makes sense if you are

  • A founder, operator, or senior leader at a B2B company
  • Dealing with a real, recurring data or outbound problem
  • Looking for clarity, not a vendor pitch
  • Ready to invest in fixing the system, not just patching it

It's probably not worth your time if you are

  • Running an RFP or comparing vendors on price
  • Looking for free consulting or a speculative proposal
  • Hoping for quick fixes over durable solutions

Next Steps

What happens after the call

If there's a fit, we'll outline a clear next step — either a scoped Data Intelligence build or a Sales Acceleration pilot, depending on where the problem lies.

If there isn't a fit, we'll say so directly — and often point you toward something more useful.

There's no obligation either way.

We'd rather have a short, honest conversation that goes nowhere than a long one that leads somewhere it shouldn't.
