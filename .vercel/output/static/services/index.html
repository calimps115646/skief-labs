Service One

Data Intelligence

The data your team needs — built, enriched, and delivered automatically.

The Problem

Most B2B teams have a data problem they've quietly learned to work around.

  • Lists that are months out of date before they're used.
  • Research that pulls senior people away from higher-value work.
  • Enrichment that's incomplete, inconsistent, or both.
  • Decisions made on inputs no one fully trusts.

This isn't a tools problem.
It's a systems problem.

The fix isn't another data subscription. It's a pipeline that finds, enriches, scores, and delivers the right information automatically — so the work stops requiring people.

What We Build

Research & Scraping Pipelines

The data your team currently sources by hand — contacts, companies, roles, signals — collected automatically from the sources that matter for your specific use case. Continuously refreshed, not exported once and forgotten.

AI-Powered Enrichment & Scoring

Raw data isn't useful. We build enrichment logic that layers context, qualifies records, and scores outputs — so what reaches your team is clean, relevant, and ready to act on.

Agentic Research Workflows

For complex research that currently consumes senior time, we build AI agents that gather, structure, and summarize — in the exact format your team uses to make decisions.

Signal & Trigger Identification

Hiring activity. Funding events. Technology changes. Leadership moves. We surface the buying signals relevant to your ICP automatically, so your team acts on fresh intelligence instead of stale assumptions.

Service Two

Sales Acceleration

More pipeline. Without more headcount.

The Problem

Most B2B outbound fails for a reason that has nothing to do with the product, the market, or the team.

It fails because the system underneath is fragile.

Manual list building. Inconsistent personalization. Follow-up that depends on someone remembering. No visibility into what's working until it's too late to fix it.

The result is a lot of effort producing unpredictable results — and a team that's busy but not productive.

We fix the system. Then we run it.

What We Do

Outbound System Design

Before a single message is sent, we design the full targeting logic, data sources, personalization framework, and sequence architecture.

Clay-Powered Targeting

We use Clay as the data backbone — building dynamic lead sources, enrichment waterfalls, and scoring logic that ensures every contact your team reaches is the right one.

Multi-Channel Sequences

Email and LinkedIn, working together. Touchpoints designed around actual buyer behavior and intent signals — not generic cadences copied from a playbook.

Done-For-You Operation

We run the outbound. Research, enrichment, personalization, sequencing, inbox management, and continuous optimization — handled by us. Your team focuses on the conversations.

Who This Is For

Sales Acceleration is built for B2B founders, sales leaders, and growth teams who know their outbound should be working harder — and who are honest about the fact that the current approach isn't scaling.

We work with a small number of Sales Acceleration clients at a time. That's not a positioning line — it's how we maintain the quality that makes the work effective.

Not a fit? This is not a fit for teams looking for a short campaign or a quick blast. This is for teams who want a reliable outbound motion they can build on.

What Good Looks Like

Our Approach

A note on AI & automation

You'll notice we don't list AI and automation as a service.
That's intentional.

AI and automation are how we build — not what we sell. Every Data Intelligence pipeline runs on automation. Every Sales Acceleration system uses AI for enrichment, personalization, and research at scale.

We don't offer automation as a standalone product because the technology is never the point. The point is the outcome — cleaner data, more pipeline, less friction for the people doing the work.

If you're looking for a partner who uses automation and AI seriously — embedded in systems designed to solve real problems — we might be exactly right.
